# The Cyberpunk RED Generator Got a Big Upgrade

The CPR random generators have been eating development time for months. Just pushed a major update. Here's what changed.

## NPCs Are Now Combat-Ready

Random NPCs now include a full combat stat block: HP, armor (current and max SP), a weapon with damage and range. Skills are role-appropriate and leveled. They get gear, a single-sentence background hook (why they matter), and a one-liner voice quote. Still as fast to generate as before, just way more usable at the table.

## Encounters Actually Have Enemies

Encounters generate a real enemy stat block now, not just flavor. HP, SP, initiative bonus, combat skill rating, weapon. Threat tiers scale the numbers. Add terrain (rooftop, alley, corporate floor), an objective (steal the data, protect the target), and a complication (reinforcements arrive, civilians nearby). Prints on a clean sheet.

## Four New Generators Live Here

Gigs: full job details with fixer name, client, target, payout in credits, main complication, and a twist that surfaces mid-run. Fixers: procedural fixer NPCs with personality. Vehicles: rides with SDP (structural), SP (armor), seat count, handling. Street Names: quick aliases for your characters.

## Everything Prints Now

Every result has a copy button. Results also have a "print sheet" button that opens a clean, printer-friendly session sheet. No browser styling to mess up the output.

Table-facing tools, better tested under fire.
